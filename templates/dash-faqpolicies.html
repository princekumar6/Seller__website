Policies FAQs

1.  What is Seventh Square Policy for lost/damaged goods or fraudulent customer complaints?

Seventh Square maintains clear policies and arbitration process for such events. Please refer to Marketplace agreement and Terms of Use for detailed understanding. You can reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com for any seller related queries and complaints.

2.  What should I do to show complete efficiency from seller’s side in case of order dispute?

We advise you to keep adequate proof that you shipped an authentic/undamaged product. This will help us arbitrate faster and better. This could include a video/photo of the product under delivery or packaging.

3.  Would I get my product price if the goods are lost in transit?

Seventh Square strives to make the marketplace a fair place for buying and selling. For any event not caused by you, which affects the transaction, you can be assured of your dues.

4.  What happens in case of a conflict with a customer?

Seventh Square team enables an efficient resolution in case of conflicts between sellers and buyers. As a seller, you’re advised to keep sufficient proof regarding your orders & payment to avoid any conflicts and ensure smooth transactions.
