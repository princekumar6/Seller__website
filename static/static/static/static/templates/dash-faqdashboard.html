Dashboard FAQs

1.  How can I update Inventory for any product?

You can go to Active Products and select edit for the product you want to update inventory for.

2.  How do I manage Orders?

You can address all your orders easily on the dashboard in your seller account under the Current Orders tab.

3.  How can I add products?

You can add products on Add New Product tab in your Seller Panel.

4.  Where do I choose my Shipping Policy with Seventh Square?

Shipping can be chosen differently for every product added and is available as an option when you’re adding a new product.
You can also edit the same for an added/existing product by going to the Active Products section in your seller dashboard.

5.  What do I do if I have any issues in listing my catalogue?

You can reach out to the Seller Help at seller@seventhsq.com with your query. They will guide you from the start regarding listing & uploading your portfolio. We also have partnered with cataloguing partners who will update your listing to make it more attractive and complete for the customers at competitive prices.

6.  If any issue, how do I reach out to SS?

You can reach out to Seventh Square’s Seller Help at seller@seventhsq.com or write to us here.
