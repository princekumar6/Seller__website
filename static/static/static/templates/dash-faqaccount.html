Account FAQs

1.  How do I register myself as a seller on Seventh Square?

Visit seller.seventhsq.com to register as a seller.

2.  How do I start selling on Seventh Square?

After registering yourself as a seller and filling out the rest of the required details & documents, you can add products and begin selling.
We’ve made the whole process simple for you. As soon as you register, you’ll see a pop-up/lightbox to fill out rest of the required information. Filling out there helps you get your account verified sooner and you won’t have to later figure out how to proceed.
We also send emails on your registered email address so you don’t have to worry about anything.

3.  Is GSTIN mandatory for Selling on Seventh Square?

Yes. As per govt. rules, GSTIN is mandatory for every seller online

4.  Is it mandatory to keep Inventory Updated?

No. But we recommend to keep it updated so that customers who are looking for the product do not face any issues. If you’re not keeping inventory updated with us, it is advised to keep reasonable inventory for every product you sell to fulfill any incoming orders.

5.  Do I need to keep account of my orders and transactions from Seventh Square?

Yes. As per Govt. norms, all sellers on e-commerce marketplaces have to keep an complete account of their sales and transactions through/with any online marketplace including Seventh Square.

6.  If any issue, how do I reach out to SS?

You can reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com or write to us directly here.

7.  Who ships the products purchased by the customer?

Seventh Square has a well defined shipping policy. As a seller, you can decide how to ship your product to the customer.
You can choose to deliver through Seventh Square or use Self-Shipping if you already have an extensive logistics network.

8.  Where do I choose my Shipping Policy with Seventh Square?

Shipping can be chosen differently for every product added and is available as an option when you’re adding a new product.
You can also edit the same for an added/existing product by going to the ‘Active Products’ section in your seller dashboard.

9.  Is there any policy, if I don't keep my inventory updated?

No. It is up to you to keep inventory updated with us. However, we do advise to keep sufficient inventory in hand for potential orders.

10.  How should I price my products to receive my desired amount?

We provide you the option to see complete pricing structure before you even start uploading your portfolio. With the help of our price calculator, you can see what the customer is paying, what we’re accounting for and what is remitted back to you. You also have the freedom to choose what the customer should pay or how much you want to receive per order.

11.  Who can see my company and bank details when filled in the account?

Only Seventh Square will have this information. It is not shared with any other partner or customer.

12.  I forgot the password for my seller account. What do I do?

Follow the ‘forgot password’ link on the login page to recover/change your password.

13.  How do I access my company’s seller account?

If your company has already registered as a seller, your company’s representative can help you with accessing the account.
