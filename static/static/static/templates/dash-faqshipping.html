Shipping & Returns FAQs

1.  What is Seventh Square’s shipping policy?

Seventh Square has put in a simple and liberal shipping policy to help seller truly control their business. You can choose to ship with Seventh Square or self-ship the products if you have an extensive logistics network. You can also choose to show how much non-refundable shipping should the customer pay except the product price.

2.  What is the process if I want to choose Self-Shipping?

If you prefer to deliver the orders by your partners/logistics network, here’s how you can proceed:

Send in your Shipping Policy/SLA to us at seller@seventhsq.com
We’ll verify it, understand it and incorporate it in our system.
After the above, we’ll notify you and enable self-shipping on the products you’ve selected for the same.

3.  How does the delivery system work for Self-Shipped orders?

As soon as the customer places and order, you’ll be notified and required to package the product, label it and put on the invoice sent by us and deliver it through your partner to the customer’s address ( which you can see on orders tab). However, since we have to keep the customer updated on delivery terms, you’ll have to regularly put in updates regarding delivery and the order, on the dashboard. These updates too can be done in the orders tab under relevant orders.

4.  Can I raise claim if the customer has sent a different product than the original product?

Yes. If accepted return from customer, but the product is not what was ordered & delivered, you can raise a claim with us regarding this.

5.  Can I use my own shipping while selling on SS?

Yes. Check in the price calculator to see how it’ll affect selling price and your payout.

6.  What happens when I can't deliver the product on time?

If the product is not delivered within acceptable delivery time, it is marked under seller cancellation. We advise you to keep inventory and delivery date & time duly updated on the website so the customer doesn’t walk away unhappy. Delivering on time helps you get positive feedback which helps your products list better and increase sales.
