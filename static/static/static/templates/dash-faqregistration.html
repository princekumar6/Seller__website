Registration FAQs

1.  How do I sell on Seventh Square?

You can register yourself as a seller at seller.seventhsq.com .After registering, you can finish up some details required to sell online and upload your portfolio. Voila! You’re ready to sell.

2.  What all details are required for Seller Registration?

Company/Representative Name, Phone number, Email and GST are required to create a free seller account with us.

3.  What documents would I need to start selling?

Government of India has mandated some KYC procedures for online selling.
Here is a list of documents you’ll need to sell with us:

  • GSTIN
  • Business & Personal PAN
  • Bank Account and Supporting verification documents
  • Company & Personal Address Proof

4.  What are the charges related to registration and product listing?

Seventh Square does not charge for registration and product listing. It’s completely free!

5.  I’m having trouble with the registration process. Can I get some help?

You can reach out to seller support at seller@seventhsq.com or arrange a callback by filling out your query at Write to us.

6.  Do I need to sign any physical agreement?

No. Your affirmation on the registration form states your acceptance of the agreement and terms. Therefore, we advise you to read all policies and agreements before registering.

7.  What are the Terms & Conditions mentioned on the registration page?

Terms & Conditions include the Seller Agreement & Marketplace policies for efficient working of the marketplace. These form the basis of the partnership between the seller and the marketplace.

8.  I have filled all the details correctly and still not able to register my account. What should I do?

If any technical issue, you can reach out to seller@seventhsq.com . They will help you register your account.
