Who we are

We are a marketplace for Home Interior products started with the aim to make shopping for your Home simpler!

How it started

It started when I wanted to help my parents build interiors for our newly constructed house in Jaipur. They wanted to shop for lights, furniture & furnishings, doors & windows and Decor. I went around at least 17 shops and multiple online stores before I finally completed Home Shopping with my family. It was exhausting and if I had to do it again, I wouldn't. That's how Seventh Square was born, so that Home Shopping could be done on a single website or an app sitting at home sipping tea.
-Aditya Agarwal
Founder

What we do

We make the Home Shopping experiences simpler & much more interesting without burning a hole in anyone's pocket! With the help of our sellers, Consumers can enjoy 1000s of products across every interior category with easy payment & shipping. We even take care of your reservations about shopping for your home online. See how the product would actually look in your space with our Design Room. Get consultation online with our Interior Designers too. Shop only after you're completely satisfied with every aspect. After ordering, we also offer installation so that you don't have to do anything.

Our Vision

Is to make Home Products shopping simple, convenient & accessible for consumers in every region of the country. We're making it happen by providing complete Home Shopping experiences on a single platform including consultancy, virtual designing & installation.
