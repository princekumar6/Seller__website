Seventh Square Business

By - Seventh Square

Seventh Square doesn’t just give you a platform to reach the end consumer. We also help sellers reach institutions, producers & professionals like Builders, Construction Firms, Architects & Interior Designers as well as small businesses who are looking to buy in bulk.

How it works?

For any bulk or business buyer, the best available catalog is sent as per their requirements from one/various sellers based on several factors including location, price, inventory, product reviews, return policy, etc.

For example, a builder in Surat, Gujarat is looking for panel lights, bathroom sinks, faucets and building materials for his under construction site. We will go through our sellers for every category and search for which sellers offer most affordable price for a particular product and where the seller is based as compared to the builder. This adds some more filters like sellers with inventory updated and flexible return policy, etc. and is finalized after discussing with the shortlisted sellers. Finally, the quote is sent along with all details and when an order is placed, we deliver those items from seller’s warehouse to the builders’ location. Follow up and after sales is done by Seventh Square’s Business Team.

Benefits to Sellers

Whether you are in Sanitaryware or Building Materials, we’ll help you reach all potential business clients within your area and across the country. Sell easily without the hassle of shipping and customer service and grow your business without any external marketing costs. We get the clients, prepare the quote, deliver the order, transfer the payment to you and be in-touch with the clients all along.

How can you register yourself as a business/bulk seller?

Seventh Square has made it easy for sellers to register and sell. For registering for B2B, send an email to seller@seventhsq.com with your business name, location and categories serviced and our team will reach out and carry it forward with you. Seventh Square business works on the backend for most transactions, so most work is finished within our offices and you can focus on growing your own business with us.
