3.  How does Selling on marketplace like Seventh Square works?

As a marketplace, Seventh Square is an intermediary for sellers and buyers. Thus, we only act as service providers and all decisions relating to product is with the seller.
As a seller, you first have to upload your products on the marketplace using Seventh Square’s dedicated seller panel (seller.seventhsq.com). When customers purchase your product on our Website, you’ll get a notification with all the details. While delivering, you can choose to ship with Seventh Square or self-ship to the customer’s address. You’ll receive payments directly to your bank account no matter how the customer chooses to pay.

5.  What are the charges related to selling on Seventh Square?

Seventh Square has the least overall charges to seller, thus actually helping you grow your business!
SS doesn’t charge any listing or registration fees, rather only when products are sold.
For every product sold, there is a marketplace commission which is charged on the final price sold to the customer. In addition, there is shipping and processing charge which are forwarded to respective partners (delivery partner and Payment Gateway).
By keeping these simple and low, we look forward to create a community of sellers, who actually benefit by selling online.

6.  How much Commission does Seventh Square takes?

We charge the lowest commission in the industry! For Sellers registering before commercial launch, they are entitled to pay just 5% marketplace commission for the first year. For Sellers registering after launch, refer to commercial term segment available in policies tab.

14.  What is the breakdown of pricing structure of SS?

Seventh Square’s marketplace fee includes:

  • Marketplace Commission, which is a fixed percentage of the total amount the customer pays. This percentage is different for different product categories. Please refer to Commercial Term Segment for detailed commission table.
  • Payment Processing, is the payment gateway or cash collection charge which is collected on total product price paid by the customer. It is fixed at 3% of total order amount.
  • Shipping Charges varies as per product weight. Seventh Square has a fixed rate shipping policy and offers you a single rate based on product weight. This is only charged on orders shipped with Seventh Square.
